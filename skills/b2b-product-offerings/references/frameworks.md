# B2B Offer Creation Frameworks

## 1. The PURE Problem Framework
An offer is only as strong as the problem it solves. A "PURE" problem has four characteristics:
- **Painful**: It causes significant friction or loss in the business.
- **Urgent**: It needs to be solved immediately to prevent further damage or missed opportunities.
- **Recognized**: The target audience is already aware that this is a problem.
- **Expensive**: The cost of inaction (in time, money, or risk) is high.

## 2. The Proprietary vs. Commoditized Offer
- **Commoditized Offer**: A service that competitors also sell (e.g., "we do SEO"). Leads to price wars and "I'll think about it" responses.
- **Proprietary Offer**: A unique solution built around a specific process or intellectual property (e.g., "The 90-Day Organic Pipeline Accelerator"). Leads to "When can we start?" responses.

## 3. The 7 Factors of a Winning Offer
1. **Market Demand**: High desire for the outcome.
2. **Painful Problem**: Solving a critical issue.
3. **Dream Outcome**: Clearly defined end-state for the client.
4. **Time Delay**: How fast they see results (lower is better).
5. **Effort & Sacrifice**: How much work they have to do (lower is better).
6. **Perceived Likelihood of Achievement**: Confidence in your ability to deliver.
7. **Scarcity & Urgency**: Why they should buy now.

## 4. Offer Stacking (The Value Multiplier)
To increase perceived value without just lowering price:
- **Core Offer**: The primary solution.
- **Bonuses**: Tools, templates, or extra services that speed up results.
- **Guarantees**: Risk reversal (e.g., "Money back if X isn't achieved").
- **Payment Terms**: Flexible options to lower the barrier to entry.

## 5. B2B SaaS Subscription Model
For software products, focus on scalability and reducing time-to-value:
- **Tiered Packaging**: Basic, Pro, and Enterprise tiers to capture different market segments.
- **Usage-Based Pricing**: Aligning cost with the actual value consumed (e.g., API calls, seats).
- **Annual vs. Monthly**: Incentivize long-term commitment with discounts.
- **Self-Service Onboarding**: Reducing friction by allowing users to see value before talking to sales.

## 6. High-Ticket Service Model
For services exceeding $10k+, focus on risk mitigation and ROI:
- **Phased Engagements**: Start with a low-risk audit or pilot program.
- **Outcome-Based Pricing**: Tying a portion of the fee to specific business results.
- **Consultative Selling**: Co-creating the solution with the client to ensure alignment.
- **White-Glove Support**: Including dedicated account management as a value multiplier.
