# B2B Product Offerings

This skill provides a structured methodology for creating "proprietary" B2B offers that solve painful, urgent, recognized, and expensive (PURE) problems. It moves away from commoditized services toward high-value, unique solutions that minimize price objections.

## Core Workflow

### 1. Identify the PURE Problem
Before structuring an offer, diagnose the target market's most critical issue.
- **Painful**: Significant business friction.
- **Urgent**: Needs immediate resolution.
- **Recognized**: The client knows they have it.
- **Expensive**: High cost of inaction.

### 2. Design the Proprietary Solution
Avoid generic service descriptions. Package the solution as a unique methodology or system.
- Name the process (e.g., "The [Name] Framework").
- Break it into 3-5 distinct phases.
- Focus on the "Dream Outcome" rather than features.

### 3. Build the Offer Stack
Multiply the perceived value by adding complementary elements.
- **Core Offer**: The primary solution.
- **Value Add-ons**: Templates, scripts, or audit tools that reduce client effort.
- **Risk Reversal**: Implement a strong guarantee to lower the barrier to entry.

### 4. Structure the Pitch
Use the provided template to document the offer clearly for the prospect.

## Best Practices
- **Lower Effort & Sacrifice**: The best offers are those where the client has to do the least amount of work to see results.
- **Decrease Time Delay**: Highlight "Quick Wins" that occur in the first 14-30 days.
- **Increase Likelihood of Achievement**: Use case studies and specific metrics to prove the offer works.
