# AI Tool SMB Researcher

This skill helps Manus research specific AI tools and find high-value YouTube content tailored for small business owners, particularly those in the home service trades.

## Core Objectives

When researching a tool, focus on answering these core questions for the SMB owner:
1. **Where do I start?** Provide a clear, first-step action.
2. **Hype vs. Reality:** Is this tool actually useful for a small shop or just overhyped?
3. **ROI:** How does it save money or generate leads? (e.g., "saves 5 hours of admin," "catches 2 more leads per week").
4. **Human Touch:** How does it support (not replace) their team?

## Workflow

### 1. Tool Research
Research the AI tool's core features, pricing, and specific use cases for SMBs. Use the `customer_research.md` reference to align findings with common SMB pain points.

### 2. Search for High-Value Content
Search for YouTube videos that demonstrate:
- Practical tutorials for non-technical owners.
- Case studies of ROI in service businesses.
- Comparisons with existing workflows (e.g., manual scheduling vs. AI).

### 3. Synthesis and Delivery
Present the findings in a professional report. Use the vocabulary from `customer_research.md` (e.g., "get your evenings back," "plug the leak") to resonate with the audience.
